Kids News Buddy - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

About Kids News Buddy

Kids News Buddy is an Alexa skill that delivers personalized, age-appropriate news to children aged 6-17. This privacy policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Kids News Buddy collects and stores minimal information to personalize your experience:

  • Topic Preferences: The 3 news topics you choose (e.g., sports, animals, technology)
  • Session Feedback: Whether you found headlines helpful (yes/no responses)
  • Session Count: Number of times you've used the skill (to provide variety in headlines)

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (names, ages, birthdates)
  • Location data or addresses
  • Contact information (email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Voice recordings
  • Payment information
  • Any other personally identifiable information

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Remember your topic preferences for future sessions
  • Provide variety in headlines across different sessions
  • Improve the skill based on aggregated feedback

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely in Amazon DynamoDB (AWS cloud service) and is associated only with your anonymous Alexa user ID. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or distribute your data to any third parties. Your information stays private and is used only within the Kids News Buddy skill.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Kids News Buddy is designed for children aged 6-17 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • Only anonymous preferences and feedback are stored
  • No registration or account creation is required
  • Parents can delete all data at any time

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Disabling the Kids News Buddy skill in your Alexa app
  • Saying "Alexa, delete everything I said today"
  • Contacting us through the Alexa Skills feedback system

When you disable the skill, all stored preferences are automatically deleted.

Third-Party Services

Kids News Buddy uses the following third-party services:

  • NewsAPI.org: For live news content (no personal data is shared)
  • Amazon DynamoDB: For secure data storage (AWS service)
  • Amazon Alexa: Voice interface platform

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures.

Content Safety

All news content is filtered for age-appropriateness using:

  • Comprehensive content blocklist (90+ inappropriate words)
  • Kid-friendly news source filtering
  • Manual curation of fallback headlines

Parental Controls

Parents can:

  • Monitor skill usage through the Alexa app
  • Disable the skill at any time
  • Delete voice recordings in Alexa Privacy settings
  • Review topic preferences with their child

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Kids News Buddy, please contact us through:

  • The Alexa Skills feedback system in the Alexa app
  • The skill's page in the Alexa Skills Store

Disclaimer

Kids News Buddy is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Amazon, Alexa, or any news organization. This is an independent educational skill created to help children learn about current events in a safe, age-appropriate way.

News content is aggregated from publicly available sources and filtered for child-appropriate content. We do not claim ownership of news content and provide proper attribution to news sources.
